Hi my beautiful friends, in this little work i’ve done i want to show how a cam works from a mathematical and physical standpoint.

So, what is a cam? A cam is a device used to safely (or somewhat) climb on routes where there’re not fixed bolts. On such climbs it is demanded to add so called “quick protection” to arrrest a fall if necessary. Since you’ve come across this blog probably you happily now what i’m talking about, here’s a image of this sexy tool:

Notice how the cam shape is made in such a way to “expand” as a force is applied on the sling. Also cams have the possibility to be open and closed as needed however the contact point is always perpendicular to the applied surface. The key of a working cam is to find that such shape that maintains a constant angle between the rock and the metal: the logaritmic spiral.
